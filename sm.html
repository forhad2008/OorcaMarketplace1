About Us

We collect professional and luxury-quality designs to help people build and grow their careers. Oorca also provides opportunities for designers to sell their products and establish themselves as professionals within the Oorca design platform. To join the Oorca brand, designers must submit an application and a resume, which can also be purchased directly from Oorca if needed. Designers who sell products on Oorca are charged a 15% commission per sale; alternatively, they may choose a one-time prepayment of $150 instead of commission. All submitted designs must meet high professional and quality standards. Customers who purchase products can request text or information edits, with a small editing fee of $1 per product.
